Welcome

This virtual museum explores the legal, political, and cultural dimensions of how East and West Germany confronted the legacy of Nazi crimes through the courts. Through selected trials, archival material, and comparative analysis, the project highlights how post-war justice was shaped by Cold War divisions and divergent ideologies.
